--------------------
Package Information

Battlers, SV Battlers and Sprites folders (Original & 2X/3X) contains 9 sprites split by the following categories:
- Female Fighter (Heroine)
- Male Fighter (Hero)
- Witch
- Elf Archer 
- Samurai Warrior
- Dancer
- Thief
- Dragon Knight
- Dragon Knight (Helmet)

Portrait folders (Original & 2X/3X) contains:
- Busts subfolder with 9 large portraits
- 2 facesets (original and alternative, with helmet Dragon Knight), with 8 portraits each.

Extras folders contains:
- SV Weapons folder, at original source side for overlay options.
- System folder with custom windowskins and SV Battler weapons for RPGMV/MZ Engines.
